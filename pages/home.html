Our Mission 💫

At Cailo Adoption Shelter, we believe every paw deserves a place to call home. Our mission is to rescue, love, and rehome animals in need, one tail at a time.

Our dedicated team of animal lovers ensures each pet feels cared for and valued from the moment they arrive.

With a shared passion for making a difference, we combine our skills, time, and hearts to match every animal with the perfect family.

How to Adopt

1. Find Your Match

Browse our available pets and choose the one that captures your heart.

2. Submit an Application

Fill out a quick reservation form so we can get to know you better.

3. Meet & Take Home

Schedule a meet-and-greet and bring your new best friend home.
